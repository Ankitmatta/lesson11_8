Spam Musubi

What is it?

Spam musubi originates from Hawaii and is an incredibly popular food. For a little bit of history, spam, canned cooked pork, became incredibly popular in Hawaii after the second world war and the Japanese Americans there created spam musubi because of it.

Chris Hackmann, Wikimedia Commons, CC BY-SA 4.0

Making it

To make it, spam musubi is a pretty simple recipe. It begins with frying or grilling sliced spam which is then put on top of rice and then wrapped with nori, or dried seaweed.

Variations

There are many variations spam musubi. The type of rice can differ, sometimes egg is added to it, and sometimes you don't even use spam. You can make musubi with any protein (chicken, shrimp, hot dog, tofu, etc.).
